UofT AI

I'm the Director of Conference at UofT AI, the University of Toronto's largest AI student group. I’m leading a team of 17 students to organize Toronto’s biggest student-run AI conference, ConferenceX. I'm coordinating between 15+ speakers from leading AI companies, startups, and academia including Google, Accenture, Citadel, the World Economic Forum, the Vector Institute, Stanford, and more. I'm also corresponding with 5 sponsoring companies like KPMG and PWC, 4 startups like TempoLabs and Perplexity, 6 research labs across Ontario, and 3 internal UofT AI teams (marketing, partnerships, ProjectX) to bring this conference to life. My ultimate goal with ConferenceX 2025 is creating a space for nuanced conversations on artificial intelligence, its applications, and global impact.
